Who I Am

My name is Timothy Mukisa, a software engineering student based in Uganda with a deep passion for building technology that creates real value. I go by TimSolutionsEng — because every great engineer is ultimately a problem solver.

I started my journey curious about how the web works and quickly fell in love with turning ideas into fully functional products.
